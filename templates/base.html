Services & Fees:

☆

  •  If you don’t already have your rough draft, I can help you organize your thoughts into a coherent essay or story.

☆

  • If you already have a rough draft, I can stylistically improve all aspects of your manuscript—structure, voice, plot deepening, and coherence—to elevate your writing and make it resonate with your target audience.

  • Fees for coaching on how to use your original ideas and voice to craft your college essay, I charge a straight fee of $1,000.
